Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a timeless comfort food that brings warmth to any gathering, with its rich layers of pasta, cheese, and savory sauce. This versatile dish can be customized to suit various tastes and dietary preferences, making it perfect for both seasoned chefs and kitchen novices alike. In this recipe, you'll learn how to create a delicious lasagna that will impress your family and friends, all while enjoying the simple pleasure of homemade cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 400g mozzarella cheese
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 tsp each parsely, salt and black pepper
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 package lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
