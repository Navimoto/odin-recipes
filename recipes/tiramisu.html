Simple Tiramisu Recipe

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that perfectly combines coffee-soaked ladyfingers with creamy mascarpone. This simple recipe captures the essence of this beloved treat, making it easy to whip up at home. With just a few ingredients and minimal effort, you can indulge in a delightful slice of heaven thatâ€™s sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 1 package ladyfingers biscuits
  • 1 1/2 cup coffee
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat yolks, sugar, and vanilla until smooth and light yellow. Fold mascarpone into yolk mixture. Set aside.
  2. Dip ladyfingers briefly in coffee and arrange 12 of them in the bottom of an 8x8-inch dish. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers. Repeat with remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone. Cover and chill 1 hour. Sprinkle with cocoa just before serving.
